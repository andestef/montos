Schematic for Binary Montos Format (BMF)
Compiled to MON (MONtos format) using moncomp filename.bmf
DEFAULT MODE
0 - NUL (Always first character)
1 - EXECUTE (Always last character otherwise treated as data file)
2 - MethodStart
3 - MethodEnd/ArgumentStart
4 - ArgumentEnd
5 - ArgumentSep
6 - ESC (Treats next value as a RAW value not as a control value)
7 - VARStart
8 - VAREnd
9 - FunctStart
10 - FunctEnd
COMMENTS: /*...*/
COMMENTNEXT: \
METHODS (Called 2 (val) 3 (arg1) 4) or (2 (val) 3 (arg1) 5 (arg2) 4) or (2 (val) 3)
0 - PRINT (VAL)
1 - VARSet (NAME,VAL)
2 - INPUT (VAR)
4 - CLEAR // NOTE: This is called as 2 4 3 Since it has no Arguments
5 - WAIT (MILLISECS,FUNCT,*ARGS)
8 - ABSPATH (RELATIVE_PATH)
9 - MKDIR (DIR_NAME)
10 - EQUALITY (A,B,VAR)
11 - IF (COND,FUNCT,*ARGS)
12 - CHMOD (TYPE: (0 - add, 1 - Subtr, 2 - Set), PERM, FILE, USR=CURRENT_USR)
NOTE: ASCII 13 (CR) maps to ASCII 10 (LF) (Dont use)
14 - READ (FILE,VAR)
15 - WRITE (FILE,TXT)
16 - APPEND (FILE,TXT)
17 - REPLACE (ORIGINAL,REPLACE,TEXT,VAR,CNT=-1)
18 - DELETE (FILE/DIR,FORCE=0)
19 - ISDIR (PATH,BOOLVAR)
20 - SYSRUN (COMMAND)
21 - ARGPARSE (NAME,TYPE...)
22 - TONUM (TEXT,VAR)
